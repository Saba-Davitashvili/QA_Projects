# [BUG-006] Footer Address Discrepancy

## Metadata
- **Project**: Professional Occupational Safety & Risk Management Platform
- **Component**: Footer Content Section
- **Type**: Bug / Content Accuracy
- **Severity**: Low
- **Priority**: Low
- **Status**: Open

## Environment Details
- **Platform**: Web
- **App Context**: Footer section on all pages

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Navigate to any page on the website.
2. Scroll to the footer section.
3. Locate the company address information.
4. Compare with the official company address on the contact page or business records.

## Actual Result
The footer contains an incorrect or outdated company address that does not match the official address used elsewhere on the website or in business documentation.

## Expected Result
The footer address must match the official company address and be kept synchronized with the primary address information displayed on the contact page and company records.
