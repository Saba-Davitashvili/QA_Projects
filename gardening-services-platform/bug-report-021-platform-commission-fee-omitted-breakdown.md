# [BUG-093] Platform Commission Fee Omitted from Price Breakdown Screen

## Metadata
- **Project**: On-Demand Gardening & Landscaping Service Platform
- **Component**: Pricing & Invoicing
- **Type**: Bug / Logic
- **Severity**: Medium
- **Priority**: Medium
- **Status**: Fixed

## Environment Details
- **Platform**: Desktop / Mobile
- **App Context**: Price Breakdown Panel

## Preconditions
Gardener is logged in and views the pricing details of an active order.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the active order details page.
2. Expand the price breakdown panel.

## Actual Result
The platform commission deduction fee is not detailed or displayed in the price breakdown.

## Expected Result
The platform commission fee deduction must be displayed as a line item in the breakdown to maintain pricing transparency for gardeners.
